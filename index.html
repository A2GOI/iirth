About IIRT Hyderabad

Constituted to fulfill critical objectives in translational scientific discovery, IIRT Hyderabad provides the infrastructure necessary to accelerate breakthrough investigations. Bridging baseline theory with operational scalability, the institute serves as an ecosystem for academic research, technical cross-training, and industry-aligned collaborative development.
